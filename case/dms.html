Carle Health Alliance

Document Management System

Product Design, UI Dev, Design System, Research

Jan 2020 - Sep 2020

Overview

As a traditional health insurance provider, a significant aspect of Health Alliance’s customer experience is interaction with PDF documents. The Document Management System is a digital solution to improve management and hosting of all documents used on public facing platforms. It also acts as a source of truth for all document metadata used across the organization.

The Problem

There wasn’t a clear or established process for maintaining documents used by all customers and shoppers. As a method of control, information and files would have to go back in forth in email threads across departments in order to keep documents updated and consistent across all endpoints.

As the volume of documents was over 1,000 and documents needed to be updated frequently, we needed to create a process and implement automation as much as possible to reduce production time and increase accuracy in delivery.

Research and Analysis

I had the unique responsibility of having to maintain and track all 1,000+ documents throughout our public sites and portals, so I had the subject matter expertise to pinpoint major system problems.

I conducted research by gathering data from code repositories and had a number of discussions with primary document coordinators about various cases in document management.

Personas

Two personas helped jumpstart our user journey process

Illinois Employees with children or other dependents enrolled in a university, community college or other local educational program

Younger, blue collared employees who plan to enter school in the near future.

Testing & Feedback

Testing was conducted throughout the entire process to identify major pain points. Prototypes created using Sketch and InVision were presented to stakeholders for early feedback to help guide each iteration.

The site was launched after numerous user pilot meetings in August of 2018. Google Tag Manager pixel tracking was included to gather user data throughout the site.

Impact

After many iterations and revisions we felt we had achieved a landing page that acted as the final point of the conversion cycle for prospective customers. We were proud of the result that gave the Simplete product digital presence to increase customer base and grow the bottom line.

Product Learnings

Product is discovered in design, not set prior.

In developing a product, rapid iteration in design and development produces the best results. This occurs best through achieving a shared understanding of the product constantly through the lifecycle with all parties involved.

I also gained valuable experience in guiding the development of a dynamic design system, which allows for rapid production and increased brand consistency.
